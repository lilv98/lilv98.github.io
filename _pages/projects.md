## [SEAM: Semantically Equivalent Across Modalities](/seam/)

A benchmark for evaluating vision-language models across semantically equivalent textual and visual representations.

**Paper:** [CoLM 2025](/files/colm2025_conference.pdf) | **Code:** [GitHub](https://github.com/CSSLab/SEAM) | **Dataset:** [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/CSSLab/SEAM)
