**Key Contributions:**
- First benchmark for evaluating VLMs with truly semantically equivalent inputs across modalities
- Systematic evaluation of 16 contemporary vision-language models
- Identification of critical failure modes: textual perception errors and visual hallucinations
- Evidence of systematic modality imbalances in current VLMs

**Collaborators:** [Difan Jiao](mailto:difanjiao@cs.toronto.edu), [Blair Yang](mailto:blair@cs.toronto.edu), [Ashton Anderson](mailto:ashton@cs.toronto.edu)
